Dr. Kwame Nkrumah

The first President of the Republic of Ghana

A portrait of Dr. Kwame Nkrumah

Here's a timeline of Dr. Nkrumah's life:

  • 1909 - Born in Nkroful, Gold Coast (now Ghana)
  • 1930 - Graduated Achimota College and started his career as a teacher at Roman Catholic junior schools in Elmina and Axim and at a Seminary.
  • 1935 - Entered Lincoln University in Pennsylvania
  • 1939 - Obtained masters degrees from Lincoln and from the University of Pennsylvania. He studied the Literature of socialism.
  • 1945 - Left the United States and went to England, where he organised the 5th Pan-African congress in Manchester.
  • 1949 - He formed the Convention People's Party
  • 1951 - Gold Coasts first general election, the CPP won, Nkrumah was released from prison to become leader of government business.
  • 1952 - Became Prime Minister of the Gold Coast
  • 1957 - Gold Coast gained independence and Nkrumah became the new nation's first prime minister
  • 1960 - Ghana became a republic and Nkrumah became its president
  • 1966 - While Nkrumah was visiting Beijing, the army and police in Ghana seized power
  • 1972 - He died of cancer in Bucharest
